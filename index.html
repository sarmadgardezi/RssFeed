Generate RSS feed for your static / HTML nad PHP Website.

RSS Feed plays an importent role in getting your whole website Articles or Web Applications to get in google more fast. You can use Rss Feedburner by #Google to link your Rss feed with Google feedburner.

Name the RSS feed file as "rss.xml" so that you can place a url like " www/domain.com/rss.xml" or " http://sarmadgardezi.com/rss.xml " so that your RSS feed would work dynamically.
